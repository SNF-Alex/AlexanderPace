Privacy Policy

YOUR PRIVACY MATTERS

Last Updated: November 7, 2025

Overview

Pong-O ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Sign In with Apple (Optional)

  • When you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier to save your game progress across devices
  • No email address or personal information is collected or stored by us
  • Apple handles all authentication securely

Game Data (Local Storage)

  • Game progress (coins, unlocked items, high scores)
  • Game settings (control preferences, selected skins/themes)
  • All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage

Cloud Sync (Optional)

  • If you sign in with Apple, your game progress may sync via iCloud
  • This is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data

Information We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking
  • We do NOT display advertisements
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies

Data Usage

Game data is used solely to provide game functionality. Your progress is saved to enhance your gaming experience. No data is transmitted to external servers (except Apple's iCloud if you enable Sign In with Apple).

Account Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Navigate to Help & Settings in the app
  • Tap "Delete Account" in the Account section
  • Confirm deletion through the prompts
  • All your game data and account information will be permanently deleted

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 12+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

  • You can delete all local game data by uninstalling the app
  • You can sign out of Apple Sign In at any time in the app settings
  • You can delete your account and all data through the in-app Delete Account feature
  • Your data will be deleted when you delete the app

Data Security

Game data is stored securely on your device. Cloud sync uses Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email if provided.

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties. Your data remains yours.

Send Us a Message

Have questions or concerns about your privacy? Send us a message and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
