About Pong-O

Pong-O brings the beloved arcade classic to your pocket with a modern twist. Whether you're a nostalgic gamer or discovering Pong for the first time, this game offers endless entertainment with its simple yet challenging gameplay.

Compete against intelligent AI opponents, unlock new visual themes, and climb the leaderboards. With intuitive touch controls and smooth animations, Pong-O delivers an authentic arcade experience optimized for mobile devices.

What's Included

  • Multiple difficulty levels for all skill types
  • Unlockable paddle skins and table themes
  • Coin system for purchasing cosmetic items
  • Achievement system to track your progress
  • High score tracking and statistics
  • Optional Sign In with Apple for cloud sync
  • Offline gameplay - no internet required
  • No ads or in-app purchases
  • Regular updates with new content
